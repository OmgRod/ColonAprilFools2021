# 170th Secret Coin (April Fools 2021)

This mod "adds" the 170th secret coin into the game.

For help with the mod, I recommend you watch the following YouTube videos:

- [April Fools Video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=h7UD0lotTVM)
- [Behind the Scenes](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=q9Bu6BZbuQ8)

## FAQ

1. Is the coin legit?

> Nope. The coin does exist in this mod, but clicking it won't increase your actual secret coin count.

2. How do I use this mod?

> Watch the video(s) above - IT'S AN APRIL FOOLS VIDEO SO DO NOT TAKE EVERYTHING COLON SAYS AS TRUE! (ignoring the parts mentioning Boomlings/email), or read instructions below!

## How to use this mod?

1. Go to the <cl>Vault of Secrets</c> and type the code `destroy`.
2. <cl>Open the basement.</c> If you freed the monster <cy>(or have enabled the mod setting to spoof the monster being freed)</c>, you will see Scratch.
3. Scratch will tell you to <cb>find RobTop</c> and give him his <cp>emblem</c>. Go to the garage (icon kit area) and click the lock.
4. There, the shopkeeper will speak to you. If you click the lock again, you will get a popup asking to enter a <cg>15 character</c> code (you do not need to buy a real Steam gift card for this section).
5. Once you entered the code, click the lock again to open <cp>RobTop's lair</c>
